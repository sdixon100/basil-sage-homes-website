State-Specific Privacy Rights

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or shared
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to limit use of sensitive personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Note: Basil & Sage Homes does not sell personal information to third parties.

Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, and Utah Residents

Residents of these states have similar rights under their respective state privacy laws, including the right to opt-out of targeted advertising and profiling.

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

Basil & Sage Homes does not sell your personal information. However, some of our website features may involve sharing data with third-party services (like analytics providers) that could be considered "sharing" under certain state laws.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of your privacy rights:

  1. Submit a Request: Contact us via email, phone, or our contact form
  2. Verify Your Identity: We may ask for information to confirm your identity
  3. Receive Confirmation: We'll acknowledge your request within 10 business days
  4. Get a Response: We'll fulfill your request within 30-45 days (depending on complexity)

Authorized Agent

You may designate an authorized agent to make privacy requests on your behalf. The agent must provide:

  • Written authorization from you
  • Proof of their identity
  • Verification of your identity
