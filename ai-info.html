At Basil & Sage Homes, we believe in being transparent about how we use artificial intelligence and automated technologies to serve you better while protecting your privacy and ensuring fair treatment.

1. How We Use AI Technology

1.1 Website Experience

We use AI-powered tools to enhance your experience on our website:

  • Property Recommendations: AI algorithms help suggest properties that match your preferences based on your browsing behavior
  • Search Optimization: Natural language processing helps you find properties using conversational search terms
  • Chatbot Assistance: Automated chatbots provide instant answers to common questions 24/7
  • Property Comparison Tools: AI-powered features help you compare properties side-by-side efficiently

1.2 Application Processing

AI assists in streamlining the rental application process:

  • Document Verification: Automated systems verify submitted documents for completeness
  • Application Screening: AI helps identify incomplete applications and missing information
  • Communication Automation: Automated emails keep you informed about your application status

1.3 Customer Service

  • Email Response Assistance: AI helps our team respond to inquiries more quickly
  • Appointment Scheduling: Automated systems help schedule property tours efficiently
  • Maintenance Request Routing: AI categorizes and routes maintenance requests to appropriate staff

2. Data Used by AI Systems

2.1 Information We Process

Our AI systems may process:

  • Website browsing patterns and property preferences
  • Search queries and interaction history
  • Communication content (emails, chat messages)
  • Application form data (only with your consent)
  • Property viewing history and saved favorites

2.2 What We Don't Use

Our AI systems do NOT use:

  • Protected class information for discriminatory purposes
  • Social media data without explicit permission
  • Biometric data
  • Information from third-party data brokers for tenant screening

3. AI and Fair Housing Compliance

Basil & Sage Homes is committed to fair housing practices:

  • Regular Audits: We regularly audit our AI systems for bias and discrimination
  • Human Oversight: All rental decisions are made by qualified human staff
  • Transparent Criteria: Our tenant selection criteria are clearly defined and consistently applied
  • Appeal Process: You can request human review of any automated decision

4. Your Rights Regarding AI

You have the right to:

  • Know: Understand when AI is being used in your interactions with us
  • Opt-Out: Request human-only interaction for sensitive matters
  • Explanation: Receive an explanation of how AI-assisted decisions were made
  • Correction: Correct any inaccurate information used by AI systems
  • Appeal: Challenge decisions made with AI assistance

5. Third-Party AI Services

We may use third-party AI services from trusted providers:

  • Analytics Platforms: Google Analytics, website optimization tools
  • Customer Service: Chatbot and email automation platforms
  • Marketing Tools: AI-powered advertising and recommendation engines

These services are bound by strict data protection agreements and privacy standards.

6. AI Training and Improvement

We may use anonymized, aggregated data to improve our AI systems:

  • Interaction patterns are analyzed to improve user experience
  • Personal identifying information is removed before analysis
  • You can opt-out of having your data used for AI training

7. Security of AI Systems

We implement robust security measures:

  • Encryption of data processed by AI systems
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls limiting who can interact with AI systems
  • Monitoring for unusual activity or potential breaches

8. Future AI Developments

As AI technology evolves, we commit to:

  • Notifying you of significant changes to our AI usage
  • Maintaining transparency about new AI features
  • Continuing to prioritize fairness and privacy
  • Seeking feedback on AI-powered features
