Our Commitment to Excellence

Basil & Sage Homes is a trusted name in residential property management in the Ladson, South Carolina area. With over 15 years of experience, we specialize in providing high-quality, move-in ready single-family homes for families and individuals.

Our mission is to make renting simple and enjoyable. We handle all aspects of property management so you can focus on what matters most - making your house a home.

From prompt maintenance responses to transparent leasing processes, we're committed to excellence in every aspect of our service.

Our History

Building Communities Since 2008

Basil & Sage Homes was founded in 2008 with a simple goal: to provide better rental experiences for families in the Ladson area. What started as a small portfolio of just a few properties has grown into a trusted property management company serving hundreds of happy residents.

Over the years, we've expanded our services and refined our processes, but our core mission remains the same - to provide quality homes and exceptional service to our community.

Today, we're proud to be one of the most respected property management companies in the region, known for our integrity, professionalism, and commitment to resident satisfaction.
